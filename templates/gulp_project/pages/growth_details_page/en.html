Vaffle User Level

What's the meaning of Vaffle User Level?

In the Vaffle community, people with different culture, life experiences, and values are sharing their vape knowledge, experience, and understanding in the communication. Based on consensus of the community, we intelligently identified and calculated the behavior of each Vaper, forming a Vaffle Uuser System consisting of three dimensions: "Basic credit", "Community contribution", and "Compliance with conventions". The changes of levels will have different benefits and rights and will affect content recommendation and empower system. We hope this system can help every vaper understand community standards, make the community better, and improve user experience in the community.

User level and e-liquid value

When you register Vaffle as a new user, you will have a default level as a new vaper, if you want to be a high-level user in Vaffle, you need to collect enough e-liquid value. E-liquid value has different requirements at every different level, the higher user level needs more e-liquid value to achieve. The subsequent increase or decrease in the value of e-liquid is calculated cumulatively with the user's behavior and updated once a day.
The user's e-liquid value is comprehensively calculated from the three dimensions of "Basic credit", "Community contribution", and "Compliance with standards". The index calculation principle for each dimension is as follows:

1. Basic Credit

Basic credit is an important part of e-liquid value, we will do the data summarization and empowerment calculations based on your account behavior in the community (completeness of personal information, history of personal account punishment), etc., to evaluate your index in the basic credit dimension to generate an e-liquid value. Which behavior may affect the Basic credit index?
Each ID corresponds to not only a Vaffle account but also a real person. Through the basic credit index, we encourage users to add their personal information and verify their emails as much as possible. At the same time, we encourage everyone to actively share e-cigarette-related information in the community and give honorary marks to the excellent answerers.
If your account information (including but not limited to the user name, avatar, personal signature, etc.) contains content that violates community standards, the violation information will be reset and your basic credit index will be reduced accordingly.
If your personal account gets banned, muted and other account-level penalties because of publishing illegal content, your basic credit index will also be reduced accordingly.

2. Community contribution index

The Community Contribution Index is a score that measures the contribution of users’ content creation. Vaffle encourages users to create high-quality original content. We will perform a qualitative evaluation of the quality algorithm model based on the content posted by each user, and then calculate the number of likes, comments, and repost, etc to export a score in the e-liquid value.

What behaviors will affect the community contribution index?

High-quality original content in the community is inseparable from the support of each creator. In order to maintain a good community atmosphere, we encourage everyone to share their knowledge, experience, and insights. Professional, credible, detailed, inspiring and high-quality content will not only be officially recommended and gain more users' attention and support but will also be reflected in the improvement of the community contributions index. Specific standards include but are not limited to (for details, please refer to the Vaffle Community Standards):
● Original high-quality content, no operations such as plagiarism, manuscript integration, or unauthorized reprinting of the content.
● Continue to create content in the professional field. Vaffle will prioritize and recommend your content according to content weight on the hot page.
● Try to express comfortable content in the community, and avoid all uncomfortable speech such as pornography and violence.
● The content is serious, deep, positive, and can generate knowledge and help other Vaffle users.
● Positive feedback t other users, such as likes, comments, and reposts, will also help your community contributions index increase.
At the same time, we will lower the community contributions index, corresponding to these accounts for acts that may mislead other users and spread low-quality content such as pornography, vulgarity, violence, and repetition.

3. Compliance with community standards index

The "Vaffle Community Standards" restricts the behavior of each user in the community, and is also bottom-line protection; based on this regulation, intentional reduction of content quality and harm to the discussion atmosphere are intolerable by the community, and the content will be deleted or banned. Gradient penalties such as speech muted, ban, etc.; we comprehensively calculate the community standards index based on each Vaffle user’s community standards.

What actions will affect the community standards index?

Your community standards index will gradually rise, complying with Vaffle community standards and avoiding irregularities. To maintain your community standards index, please also avoid the following violations:
● Publish spam information: users publish content that affects user experience, disrupts the order of the community, or conduct related behaviors for the purpose of promotion.
● Malicious behaviors: abuse of product functions, conduct behaviors that affect user experience, endanger the security of the platform, and damage the rights and interests of others.
● Illegal posting: The post contains content that violates a specific clause of the community standards, such as pornography, violence, and sales of contraband.
